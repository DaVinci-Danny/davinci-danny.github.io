Overview


This website thesis explores the damage that energy production deals to the planet. I conduct an experiment on the destructive path of electronics on soil health and plant life, and conclude that electronics have severe damaging effcts on plant life. I can safely conclude that the results of energy production have very negitive affects on the planet and that we need to be more conscious of what we create and consume.
