The Experiment

A Quick Overview

In this experiment I will be testing how batteries can affect plant growth and by exactly how much. I will be placing

Materials

  • Plastic container
  • Lighter
  • Lighter fluid
  • Tree bark
  • Cotton
  • Rock
  • Carbon dioxide reader
  • Safety goggles
  • Lab coat

Method

  1. Set up 3 containers upside down.
  2. Place the carbon dioxide reader inside the containers.
  3. Place the bark in one the containers.
  4. Place the cotton wool in another container.
  5. Place rock in another container.
  6. Light each item on fire and burn for 30 seconds.
  7. Record the carbon dioxide levels on the reader.
