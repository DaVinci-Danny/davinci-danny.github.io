The Experiment

A Quick Overview

In this experiment I will be testing how batteries can affect plant growth and by exactly how much. I will be placing

Materials

  • 2 batteries
  • 2 pot plants
  • Water
  • Dirt
  • Watering container(i.e. water bottle with holes in the top)
  • Hammer

Method

  1. Place both pot plants in identical conditions in the sun.
  2. Smash the batteries but only enough to let out juice.
  3. Put the batteries in one of the watering containers.
  4. Put the water in the containers and water both plants daily.
  5. Continue for ten days, while recording growth.
  6. On the tenth day take measurements as usual and end the experiment.
